## FLOOF Committees Application

With a member count of over **200** and counting, the demands of FLOOF are ever growing. This year, the Eboard has decided launch FLOOF Committees! 

Open to current members and alumni, joining a committee means working alongside Eboard to maintain and promote FLOOF. It's an excellent way to become more involved in FLOOF as a member, as well as gain relevant experience for the committees' respective Eboard positions!

We'll be starting out with two committees of 2-4 people this semester:

### Art Committee

Members of the Art Committee help on the visual side of FLOOF, with tasks potentially including but not limited to:

 - Creating general FLOOF Ads to be put around campus, not specific to any upcoming club event
 - Help the Social Media Manager and Historian create graphics for social media posts or newsletters
 - Assisting with graphics or art in event-specific ads
 - Creating merchandise designs such as stickers, pins, and prints
 - Hosting and delivering art giveaways for server members

### Fundraising Committee

Members of the Fundraising Committee help on the financial side of FLOOF, with tasks potentially including but not limited to:

 - Discovering and contacting local businesses for fundraising event opportunities
 - Deciding on where funds should be placed and creating saving goals
 - Keeping track of raised/spent money
 - Finding/deciding on local charities and causes to donate funds to
 - Being in contact with the Ad Manager/Art Committee to spread the word on upcoming fundraising events

> <cite>Vincent (Art and Fundraising Committee forms respectively)</cite>

As of this writing, the forms for the committees will have closed, and their respective Eboard positions are in the process of evaluating all applicants,  conducting interviews if necessary. We thank you all for your continued interest and support!
